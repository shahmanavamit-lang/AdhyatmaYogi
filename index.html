Even as a young boy, his soul was pure and filled with a deep spiritual calling. At just eight years old, Param Pujya Aacharya Shrimad Vijay LabhdhiVallabh SuriswarJi MarajSaheb chose the sacred path of Diksha.

The Journey of Siddhavad
Chaturmas

Siddhavad Chaturmas was a calm and highly disciplined spiritual gathering where more than 1100 seekers lived a life of simplicity and inner focus. With silence and complete detachment from mobile phones, the entire environment remained peaceful and distraction-free, allowing everyone to turn inward and experience true spiritual depth.

The presence of Param Pujya Aacharya Shrimad Vijay LabhdhiVallabh SuriswarJi MarajSaheb was the guiding force of the entire Chaturmas. Through his powerful and regular discourses, he continuously inspired and directed the seekers on the path of discipline, humility and devotion. Under his guidance, the whole atmosphere became deeply spiritual and every participant experienced a meaningful inner transformation.

Mirpur Chaturmas

Mirpur Chaturmas is an upcoming spiritual retreat set in the serene and sacred premises of Mirpur Jain Tirth, known for its calm and spiritually uplifting environment. The space naturally supports silence, simplicity and deep inner focus, making it ideal for seekers looking to step away from daily distractions.

Under the guidance of Param Pujya Aacharya Shrimad Vijay Labhdhivallebh Suriswarji Marajsaheb, the Chaturmas is centered around discipline, self-reflection, and inner transformation. With a structured and peaceful routine, it offers a meaningful opportunity to reconnect with oneself and experience true spiritual depth.
